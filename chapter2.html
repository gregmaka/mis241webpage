Summary

Chapter 2 dove a little deeper into actual computer programming. The chapter started out introducing built-in functions, like alert and document.write. We were introduced to the different datatypes, how to create and use variables, and which keywords to avoid while naming our variables.

The chapter also showed us how to work with datatypes and variables using basic arithmetic. The importance of the order of operations, how to concactenate numbers and strings, and how to change the values in a variable.

Arrays were also discussed in this chapter. How to add an item to the end or beginning of an array. Deleting and locating items within an array. It also touched on objects and the importance of proper commenting.
